Daily

The X-axis denotes the hours of the day while the Y-axis denotes the Tasks/activities in your day as well as any subtasks, and each bar denotes the time taken by that specific task. The main tasks are represented with a colored banner and all subtasks are represented under it. Hovering over a task or subtask can display the exact start and end times for that task (in the event that the tasks don't begin at times marked on the X-axis)

Weekly

The X-axis represents the days of the week and the Y-axis represents the hours of each day. Tasks and events are placed in the respective time slot and are color coded with labels. Overlapping events are placed next to each other and on hover, each event can display more information about that event. Assuming the events to be classes of the day, hovering over any class will give you information about the professor and which classroom the class is held in. We could have a button that lets you select your course (and electives) and shows only the classes your course has.
